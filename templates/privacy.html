Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 9, 2025

Welcome to Mentics! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Mentics ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our website and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Service includes:

Personal Data You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name and email address. If you register using a password, we store a secure, hashed version of your password. If you register using Google OAuth, we collect your name and email from your Google profile.
  • Profile and Educational Data: To personalize your experience, we collect information you provide during onboarding or in your profile, such as your academic goals, learning style, anxieties, GPA, SAT/ACT scores (current and desired), strengths, weaknesses, test dates, grade level, interested majors, and target colleges. You may also voluntarily upload a profile picture.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect content you voluntarily create and submit. This includes:
    • Posts and replies you make in our community forum.
    • Your conversations with our AI Assistant.
    • Essays and associated prompts that you submit for AI analysis.

Data We Collect Automatically

  • Derivative Data: Our servers automatically collect information when you access the Services, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you have viewed.
  • Activity Data: We log your interactions with the Services, such as when you complete tasks, generate a new path, or update your stats, to power features like your activity feed and progress charts.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected about you to provide a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Generate highly personalized learning paths for test prep and college planning based on your specific goals, scores, and self-reported strengths and weaknesses.
  • Provide contextual and relevant assistance through our AI-powered chat and essay feedback tools.
  • Operate and moderate community features like the forum and leaderboard.
  • Track your progress over time and visualize your growth on your dashboard and tracker pages.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve our Services, including the performance of our AI models.
  • Administer gamification features, including awarding points for task completion and tracking daily streaks to keep you motivated.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We are committed to maintaining your trust, and we do not sell your personal information. We may share information we have collected about you in certain limited situations:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others, we may share your information as permitted or required by law.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We share data with third-party vendors who perform services for us. This includes:
    • Google Gemini API: To power our AI features, we send your inputs (such as chat messages and essay text) to Google's Generative AI services. This data is used to generate responses and is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policies.
  • Third-Party Advertisers: We do not currently share user information with third-party advertisers. If this practice changes in the future, we will update this policy and provide you with the choice to opt out.

4. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Our services are intended for high school students, who may be under the age of 18 but are generally over the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If we determine that a user is under 13, we will require verifiable parental consent before they can use the Service. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information and how it is used:

  • Account Information: You may review or change the information in your account or terminate your account at any time by accessing your account settings page.
  • Emails and Communications: If you no longer wish to receive emails or other communications from us, you may opt-out by contacting us at the email address provided below.

6. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.
